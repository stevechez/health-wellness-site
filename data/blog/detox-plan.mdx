# Introduction

Are you feeling sluggish, bloated, or just generally not yourself lately? It may be time for a detox! Detoxing can help rid your body of toxins and improve your overall health and wellbeing. In this article, we'll provide you with a 7-day detox plan, complete with daily meal plans and recommended foods and supplements.

### **Day 1:** Fresh Start

On the first day of your detox, focus on fresh, whole foods such as fruits, vegetables, lean protein, and whole grains. You can also incorporate detoxifying supplements such as milk thistle, dandelion root, and turmeric.

Breakfast: Oatmeal with blueberries and almonds
Snack: Sliced cucumber with hummus
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with roasted sweet potato and broccoli
Snack: Green smoothie (spinach, kale, apple, and ginger)
Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa and steamed asparagus

### **Day 2:** Hydration Station

Staying hydrated is key to any detox. On day 2, focus on drinking plenty of water, herbal tea, and coconut water. You can also add some lemon or cucumber to your water for added detox benefits.

Breakfast: Green smoothie (spinach, pineapple, and coconut water)
Snack: Sliced apple with almond butter
Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with sliced carrots and cucumber
Snack: Herbal tea with lemon
Dinner: Roasted chicken breast with brown rice and saut√©ed spinach

### **Day 3:** Meatless Monday

Eliminating meat from your diet can help give your digestive system a break and reduce inflammation. On day 3, focus on plant-based foods such as legumes, nuts, and vegetables.

Breakfast: Chia seed pudding with mixed berries
Snack: Rice cake with almond butter and sliced banana
Lunch: Lentil soup with a side salad
Snack: Green smoothie (spinach, banana, and almond milk)
Dinner: Roasted vegetables with quinoa and a side of steamed edamame

### **Day 4:** Juice Day

Juicing can help provide your body with a concentrated dose of nutrients while giving your digestive system a break. On day 4, focus on drinking fresh juices throughout the day.

Breakfast: Green juice (kale, celery, cucumber, and lemon)
Snack: Carrot juice with ginger
Lunch: Beet and apple juice
Snack: Green juice (spinach, cucumber, apple, and ginger)
Dinner: Creamy avocado and spinach soup

### **Day 5:** Protein Power

Protein is essential for building and repairing tissues in the body. On day 5, focus on lean protein sources such as chicken, fish, and legumes.

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with spinach and mushrooms
Snack: Protein shake with almond milk and banana
Lunch: Grilled chicken salad with mixed greens and avocado
Snack: Sliced turkey with sliced cucumber
Dinner: Baked cod with quinoa and roasted vegetables

### **Day 6:** Smoothie Day

Smoothies are a great way to incorporate a variety of nutrient-dense foods into your diet. On day 6, focus on drinking smoothies throughout the day.

Breakfast: Green smoothie (kale, banana, and almond milk)
Snack: Berry smoothie (mixed berries and coconut milk)
Lunch: Tropical smoothie (pineapple, mango, and coconut water)
Snack: Green smoothie (spinach, apple, and almond milk)
Dinner: Creamy avocado and cucumber soup

### **Day 7:** Celebration Day

Congratulations, you made it to day 7! On the last day of your detox, focus on celebrating your accomplishments.
